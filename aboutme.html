Originally from Minneapolis, MN, I developed a passion for technology early on, fascinated by the workings of video games and computers. At age 12, I moved to the small town of Elbow Lake, MN, where I attended high school and took my first HTML class, sparking my interest in coding.

However, with only one coding class offered, I eventually drifted away from it. After graduating high school, I pursued an associate degree and began a bachelor's program in Business Management at Minnesota State University of Moorhead. During this time, I accepted a management position at The Boiler Room in downtown Fargo and decided to leave school.

After ten years in management, I realized it wasn't my true calling. While considering a career change, I seized the opportunity to attend Emerging Digital Academy, where I rediscovered my passion for coding and found my ideal career path. During my time at the academy, I worked on various projects, including PantryPal, my first application, which helped me hone my skills in React, SQL, and JavaScript.

In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym, reading, playing tabletop and video games, golfing, and, of course, coding. I currently live in Fargo with my amazing wife, our two dogs, and our two cats. As I continue my journey in the tech industry, I aspire to get certified in multiple programming languages and cybersecurity, leveraging my skills and experiences to make a meaningful impact.
